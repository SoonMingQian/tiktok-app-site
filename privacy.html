Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

ShipFaster ("we", "us", or "our") is a personal content creation and scheduling tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you connect your social media accounts and use ShipFaster to schedule and publish content.

ShipFaster is a personal-use tool. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for commercial purposes.

Information We Collect

When you connect a social media account to ShipFaster, we collect and store:

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens — used to post content to connected platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, etc.) on your behalf
  • Platform user IDs and account identifiers — to associate tokens with the correct account
  • Video metadata — titles, descriptions, tags, and scheduled publish times you provide when creating content
  • Upload history and logs — records of what was published, when, and to which platform, for your own reference

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to operate ShipFaster's core functionality:

  • Authenticating your connected social media accounts via OAuth 2.0
  • Uploading videos and metadata to platforms on your behalf at scheduled times
  • Automatically refreshing expired access tokens using stored refresh tokens
  • Maintaining upload logs for debugging and your own records

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, analytics sold to third parties, or any purpose other than operating the tool for your personal use.

Token Storage & Security

OAuth tokens are stored locally in environment files (e.g. .env) on your own machine or server. ShipFaster does not transmit your tokens to any third-party server other than the platforms you have explicitly authorised (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, etc.).

You are responsible for the security of the device and environment in which ShipFaster runs.

Third-Party Platforms

ShipFaster connects to the following third-party platforms via their official APIs. Your use of these platforms is also governed by their own privacy policies:

  • YouTube / Google — policies.google.com/privacy
  • TikTok — tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Instagram / Meta — privacycenter.instagram.com/policy
  • ElevenLabs — elevenlabs.io/privacy

Data Retention

ShipFaster retains your tokens and upload logs only as long as they are needed to operate the tool. You can revoke access at any time by:

  • Disconnecting ShipFaster from your platform's security or app settings (e.g. Google account permissions, TikTok connected apps)
  • Deleting the .env file and any stored token files on your machine

GDPR — Your Rights (EU/EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to erasure — you can request deletion of your data at any time
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to restriction — you can ask us to limit how we process your data
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at qianbot02@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. ShipFaster is operated from Ireland and processes data in accordance with GDPR requirements.

Children's Privacy

ShipFaster is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of ShipFaster after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
