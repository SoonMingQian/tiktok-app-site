Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

ShipFaster ("we", "us", or "our") operates as a personal content scheduling and automation tool. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use ShipFaster to connect your social media accounts and schedule content.

ShipFaster is a personal-use tool. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes.

Information We Collect

When you connect a social media account to ShipFaster, we collect and store:

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens — used to post content on your behalf to connected platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, etc.)
  • Platform user IDs and account identifiers — to associate tokens with the correct account
  • Video metadata — titles, descriptions, tags, and scheduled publish times that you provide when creating content
  • Upload history and logs — records of what was published, when, and to which platform

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to operate ShipFaster's core functionality:

  • Authenticating your connected social media accounts via OAuth 2.0
  • Uploading videos and metadata to platforms on your behalf at scheduled times
  • Refreshing expired access tokens automatically using stored refresh tokens
  • Logging upload history for your own reference and debugging purposes

We do not use your information for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond operating the tool for your personal use.

Token Storage & Security

OAuth tokens are stored locally in environment files (e.g. .env) on your own machine or server. ShipFaster does not transmit your tokens to any third-party server other than the platforms you have explicitly authorised (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, etc.).

You are responsible for securing the environment in which ShipFaster runs.

Third-Party Platforms

ShipFaster connects to the following third-party platforms via their official APIs. Your use of these platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • YouTube / Google — policies.google.com/privacy
  • TikTok — tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Instagram / Meta — privacycenter.instagram.com/policy
  • ElevenLabs — elevenlabs.io/privacy

Data Retention

ShipFaster retains your tokens and upload logs only as long as they are needed to operate the tool. You can revoke access at any time by:

  • Disconnecting the app from your platform's security settings (e.g. Google account permissions, TikTok app management)
  • Deleting the .env file containing your stored tokens

Children's Privacy

ShipFaster is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of ShipFaster after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
