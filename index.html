// features

Everything in the pipeline.

01 ──

AI Script Generation

Paste a topic. Get a complete hook, script, and scene plan — tuned for short-form retention and optimised for your niche.

02 ──

Automated Voiceover

ElevenLabs and Edge TTS integration with word-level timestamp syncing. No recording booth needed.

03 ──

Remotion Video Render

React-based animated components render 1080×1920 Shorts at 30fps — code scenes, charts, stats, typewriter effects and more.

04 ──

Multi-Platform Publish

OAuth-connected uploads to YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram with scheduled posting times. One command to go live.

05 ──

Competitor Intelligence

Velocity-based outlier detection scans 10+ competitor channels to surface trending topics before they peak.

06 ──

Manifest-Driven

Every video is defined by a JSON manifest. Change the data, re-render instantly. No code changes for new content.

// workflow

From topic to published.
Under 10 minutes.

01

Research & Topic Selection

The weekly planner scans trending AI topics, checks YouTube saturation, and queues 6 high-confidence topics every 3 days.

02

Hook + Script + Scene Plan

AI generates a scroll-stopping hook, a full prose script (~150 words), and a scene-by-scene visual plan — all in one pass.

03

Audio Sync & Render

Voiceover is generated with word timestamps. Remotion syncs animations to speech — elements reveal exactly as they're spoken.

04

Schedule & Publish

The orchestrator uploads the rendered MP4 to connected platforms with metadata, captions, and a scheduled publish time.
